The Chronos Saga

Time is a weapon. History is written by the victors.

In a world where time travel is a reality but heavily regulated, a rogue agency discovers a conspiracy threatening to unravel the very fabric of history. Follow a team of elite temporal agents as they jump through different eras, battling shadowy figures and trying to preserve the timeline, or perhaps, rewrite it. Each season delves into a new historical period and a new facet of the overarching mystery.

Release Year: 2023

Genre: Sci-Fi, Thriller, Historical Fiction

Creator: Ava Sharma

Starring: Liam Vance, Dr. Evelyn Reed, Kaelen Thorne

Seasons: 3
